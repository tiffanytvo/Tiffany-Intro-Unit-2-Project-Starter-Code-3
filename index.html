Our Mission

In 2021, San Francisco has reported a 500% increase in hate crimes against Asians compared to 2020. This is an extremely alarming rate of increase, and it still continues to get worse. Our mission is to spread awareness and support the AAPI community.

News and Events

Yik Oi Huang

In 2019, an 88 year old woman named Yik Oi Huang was brutally beaten in the Visitacion Valley Playground (now renamed to the Yik Oi Huang Peace and Friendship Park). She was in the hospital until she died in 2020. Her family has set up a GoFundMe page for her which you can donate to here.


Lili Xu

In August 2022,a 60 year old dentist named Lili Xu was shot and killed as she was stepping out of a car. She was killed in Little Saigon, more specifically the 1000 block of 5th Avenue. Her boyfriend and the suspect have been arrested for being involved in the homicide.

Unfortunately, there are many more people and elderly Asians who have been robbed or killed while minding their own business. There are more awful accounts of these events happening not only in the Bay Area, but in the US.
